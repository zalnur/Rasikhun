# Shared-part candidates may be inferred from the Quran corpus

There is no available complete mutashabihat dataset, so Shared-Part Questions may be generated by scanning repeated wording in the active Selection. The curated Groups remain valuable evidence and receive ranking weight, but they are not the ceiling for corpus-derived shared-part questions.

## Consequences

- Candidate Quality is heuristic, not a scholarly classification.
- The scorer prefers concise, distinctive repeated wording with a manageable Location set, especially repeated verse endings.
- A later curated dataset can replace or augment the heuristic without changing answer correctness, because correctness is still Location-keyed.
